About the Firm

More Than a
Law Practice —
An Institution

Saad El-Din Law Firm & Legal Consultancy was established to embody vision, professionalism, and a global mindset. From its headquarters in Cairo, the firm extends its expertise to regional and international clients.

Founded by Counselor Ahmed Saad El-Din, a distinguished name in the Egyptian and Arab legal landscape, the firm has built its reputation through decades of complex litigation, international arbitration, and cross-border corporate transactions.
